Pizza Recipe

The Perfect Snack

This Easy Mini Pizza Recipe is a perfect back to school snack. When the kids come home after school, they're usually hungry. This quick and easy mini pizza snack is the perfect way to fuel your kids and keep them satisfied until dinner.

Ingredients

  • garlic clove minced or pressed
  • olive oil
  • crushed tomatoes in rich puree
  • brown sugar
  • Italian seasoning
  • dried basil
  • salt
  • crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. First preheat your oven.
  2. Place tortillas on a baking sheet (using parchment paper under your pizzas can make cleanup a bit easier.)
  3. Next spread pizza sauce evenly onto each tortilla, leaving a small border around the edge.
  4. Then sprinkle your desired amount of cheese on top of the pizza sauce.
  5. Top each pizza with the mini pepperoni and basil.
  6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 8-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy.
